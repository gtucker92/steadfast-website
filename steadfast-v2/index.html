Everything Tampa Bay Homeowners Want to Know

From Florida humidity and deep cleans to Airbnb turnovers, listing prep, and how we work: the answers homeowners across the greater Tampa Bay area ask us most.

How often should I deep clean my Tampa home given Florida's humidity?

In Tampa's subtropical climate, we recommend deep cleaning every 3 to 4 months rather than the typical 6-month schedule used in drier regions. High humidity accelerates mold and mildew growth in bathrooms, kitchens, and laundry areas, while year-round pollen and salt air buildup affect baseboards, window tracks, and HVAC vents. Homes with pets, allergies, or proximity to the bay or Gulf may benefit from quarterly deep cleans to keep allergens and moisture-related grime under control.

What neighborhoods in the Tampa Bay area do you serve?

Steadfast Cleaning Co. serves residential clients across Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Brandon, Lutz, Wesley Chapel, Land O' Lakes, Riverview, and Odessa. We specialize in South Tampa, Hyde Park, Davis Islands, Bayshore Beautiful, Palma Ceia, Beach Park, Westchase, Carrollwood, New Tampa, Fishhawk Ranch, Town 'N' Country, and Apollo Beach.

Do you offer Airbnb and short-term rental turnover cleaning?

Yes. We provide dedicated Airbnb and short-term rental turnover cleaning across Tampa Bay, including same-day turnovers for hosts with back-to-back bookings. Our turnover service includes linen handling, bathroom and kitchen reset, restocking of host-supplied amenities, and photo documentation between guests.

What is the difference between a standard cleaning and a deep cleaning?

A standard recurring clean maintains an already-clean home: dusting, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen and bathroom surfaces. A deep clean is more thorough and reaches areas standard cleans don't, including baseboards, light fixtures, vents, grout, appliance exteriors, and detailed scrubbing of bathrooms and kitchens. Most homes need a deep clean as the first service, and then again every 3 to 4 months in Tampa's humid climate.

Are you licensed, insured, and bonded?

Yes. Steadfast Cleaning Co. is licensed, bonded, and insured. We're a Florida LLC with general liability insurance and a janitorial bond. We carry the bond specifically because it protects clients in the rare event of theft or property damage, and it is especially important for Airbnb hosts, real estate agents, and property managers who entrust us with access to their properties.

Do you offer move-in and move-out cleaning?

Yes. Our move-in and move-out cleaning is a top-to-bottom deep clean designed for empty homes. It includes inside cabinets and drawers, inside oven and refrigerator, interior windows, wall spot cleaning, baseboards, blinds, window sills, all bathrooms and kitchens, and detailed floor work. We work with homeowners, renters, real estate agents preparing listings, and property managers turning over rentals.
