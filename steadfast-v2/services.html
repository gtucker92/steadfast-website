First Clean Detail
About Deep Cleaning

A Deep Clean is a top-to-bottom detail cleaning recommended for first-time clients, homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in 60+ days, or homes that need more than maintenance-level work. We determine whether a Deep Clean is needed during a brief walk-through before quoting. There's no separate booking for it. Clients book a One-Time or Recurring cleaning, and we recommend the right scope based on the home's condition.

A Deep Clean includes everything in a standard cleaning, plus:

  • Baseboards, door frames, and light switches
  • Vents, blinds, and ceiling fans
  • Appliance exteriors (stove, fridge, microwave, dishwasher fronts)
  • Detailed bathroom scrubbing including tile grout and fixtures

Pricing

Recurring clients: First visit is a Deep Clean, priced at 1.5x to 2x your recurring rate. From visit two onward, you're at the regular recurring rate.
One-Time clients: If your home needs Deep Clean scope, the quote reflects the additional work required.
