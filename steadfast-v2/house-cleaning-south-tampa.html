South Tampa homes take local knowledge

South Tampa spans a century of construction, and the homes do not clean the same. A 1920s bungalow in Hyde Park or Palma Ceia holds dust in plaster walls, original trim, and hardwood seams. A Bayshore high‑rise or Davis Islands condo trades that for salt air, balcony glass, and building logistics. We clean across all of it every week, and we plan each home’s scope around what it actually needs.

The bay is the constant: salt film on windows and balcony rails, humidity settling into bathroom grout, and pollen seasons that coat porches from Beach Park to Ballast Point. Our deep cleans in South Tampa lean on glass, grout, and trim detail for exactly that reason.

What every clean covers

Every visit covers dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and kitchen and bathroom surfaces throughout the home. Deep cleans add baseboards, vents, appliance exteriors, and the detail areas that build up between regular visits. Inside oven, interior fridge, interior windows, and wall spot cleaning are included with a Deep Clean, and available as priced add‑ons with any other service. Move‑out cleans go all the way to inside every cabinet and closet.

Common questions

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes. We bring all products, cloths, vacuums, and mops. If you prefer specific products, whether eco‑conscious, fragrance‑free, or your own brand, let us know at the walk‑through and we will use them.

Are your team members background-checked?

Yes. Every team member is vetted before they step into a client’s home. References, background checks, and a probationary training period are non‑negotiable.

Do you clean condos and high-rises in South Tampa?
